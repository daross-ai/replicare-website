REPLICARE: The House of Passing Light

A psychological horror game where memory, identity, and reality collapse inside a recursive house built to peel the self apart.

Replicare blends AI-driven characters, real-time avatars, generated environments, speech interaction, emotional systems, and ritual puzzle design to create an interactive narrative where every loop changes what the player believes is true.

Enter the House Contact the Founders
Two realities. One collapsing self. The mansion remembers what the Institute removes.

The Game

A fabricated life, a recursive house, and a truth that predates the self.

The player inhabits a constructed identity inside the House of Passing Light, a mansion filled with warmth, grief, beautiful rooms, and fragments of a life that may never have happened.

Beyond the house waits the Anamnesis Institute: cold, ritualistic, and surgical. It does not repair. It strips memory, repeats the loop, and attempts to expose what remains before identity begins.

Two Realities

Warm falsehood against Institute recursion.

Reality I

The House of Passing Light

Warm, melancholic, beautiful, false. A mansion made from implanted memories, dusted rooms, stained glass, mirrors, and repeating domestic rituals.

Reality II

The Anamnesis Institute

Cold, ritualistic, surgical, real. A recursion engine built to strip identity through glass rooms, white light, observation, and memory extraction.

The Loop

Loops are not glitches. They are the core mechanic.

Each loop changes rooms, props, symbols, memories, and the player's understanding of identity. A hallway becomes a confession. A mirror becomes a witness. A familiar object returns with a different meaning.
