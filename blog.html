Inside the Loop: Building a House That Remembers

The House of Passing Light is being designed as a place where architecture, identity, and memory collapse into the same playable system. These notes outline the major pillars currently shaping the game.

  • Building the House of Passing Light: warm rooms, stained glass, and implanted memories as playable architecture.
  • Loops Are Not Glitches: rooms repeat, objects shift, and meaning mutates between runs.
  • Designing the Physician: a calm antagonist whose surgical theology treats memory as a prison around the Origin Light.
  • Emotional Gauges as Gameplay: Denial, Memory, Joy/Loss, Reflection, and Acceptance shape perception and progression.
  • AI Characters and Memory: characters remember contradictions, respond to speech, and shift across loops.
  • Diffusion Models for Game Environments: generated scenes help find uncanny rooms, Institute spaces, props, and ritual imagery.
