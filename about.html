About Replicare AI

An AI game studio building horror from memory, speech, avatars, and recursion.

Replicare is focused on The House of Passing Light, an interactive psychological horror game where generated environments, real-time characters, and adaptive narrative systems make identity unstable.

What We're Building

A recursive identity thriller shaped by player choice.

The House of Passing Light moves between a warm mansion of fabricated memory and the Anamnesis Institute, a cold mechanism for stripping identity. The project combines ritual puzzle design, AI characters, speech interaction, emotional gauges, and adaptive scenes.

Replicare's prior technical foundation included immersive simulation, avatar systems, emotion detection, AI dialogue, speech systems, and interactive 3D scenes. The current focus is now an AI-driven psychological horror game built from those capabilities.

Why AI Belongs in Narrative Games

Because horror changes when the game can listen.

AI dialogue, speech systems, and emotion-aware interaction allow characters to respond to the player as a participant in the ritual rather than a visitor moving through fixed content.

Our Creative Direction

Cinematic, strange, elegant, and recursive.

The visual language blends mansion warmth, stained glass, dust, mirrors, Institute glass, surgical light, memory decay, and ritual symbols around the Witness Eye and the Origin Light.

From Simulation to Psychological Horror

Interactive systems now serve mystery, dread, and identity collapse.

The studio's foundation in real-time avatars, generated scenes, and responsive interaction has been redirected into a game where every loop rewrites the player's understanding of the self.
